Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

At CLLOVA AI OS, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI operating system and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Encrypted password

1.2 Usage Data

  • Voice command transcripts (processed locally)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error reports

1.3 Device Information

  • Hardware specifications
  • OS version
  • Network configuration

2. How We Use Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve voice recognition accuracy
  • Develop new features
  • Ensure system security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Important: Voice data is processed locally on your device by default. Cloud processing requires explicit opt-in.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Service Providers: For infrastructure and analytics (with strict data processing agreements)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of data processing
  • Export your data
  • Lodge complaints with authorities

Exercise these rights by emailing privacy@cllova.ai.

5. Security Measures

We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Regular security audits
  • Anonymization of analytics data
  • GDPR and CCPA compliance frameworks

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to registered users
  • Website announcements
